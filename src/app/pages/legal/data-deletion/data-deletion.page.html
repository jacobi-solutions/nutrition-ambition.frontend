Your Right to Data Deletion

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You have the right to request that we delete all personal information associated with your Nutrition Ambition account at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please email us at:

hello@nutritionambition.app

Please include the following information in your request:

  • The email address associated with your Nutrition Ambition account
  • A clear statement that you want your data deleted
  • (Optional) Any specific concerns or feedback about why you're requesting deletion

What Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete:

  • Account Information: Your email address, authentication credentials, and profile data
  • Nutrition Data: All food logs, meal entries, and nutrient tracking data
  • Personal Settings: Your targets, preferences, and customizations
  • Usage Data: Analytics and activity logs associated with your account
  • Communications: Any support tickets, feedback, or messages you've sent

Processing Timeline

We will:

  • Acknowledge your request within 48 hours
  • Verify your identity to protect your data
  • Complete the deletion process within 30 days
  • Send confirmation once your data has been permanently deleted

Important Notes

  • Irreversible Action: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose all your nutrition history, custom foods, and settings.
  • Legal Requirements: We may retain certain information if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, legal compliance).
  • Backup Systems: Deleted data may persist in backup systems for up to 90 days before being permanently removed.
